Tissue camera

  • Ray simulation at https://phydemo.app/ray-optics/simulator/.
    • Saved simulation (to be used at the web site above):
      tissue camera past objective simulation.json
      • The first simulation shows the rays after the objective (when they are already collimated). The first lens in the first simulation is Thorlabs LA1509-A which is currently installed at the entrance into the red channel arm of the microscope (can be replaced with the LA1509-AB version for a better signal in the third red channel at a cost of a worse signal in the first two channels). The lens in the simulation is modeled based on the curvatures and thickness of the Thorlabs lens. The last lenses in both simulations are ideal lenses with 25mm focal length. In the second simulation, the first lens is meant to mimic the objective. However, choosing 2mm focal distance (as in the Olympus XLUMPLFLN20XW objective) made the positioning complicated, and so here it's 10mm.
